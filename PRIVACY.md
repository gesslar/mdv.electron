# Privacy Policy

- Effective Date: April 29, 2026
- Application: mdv
- Publisher: gesslar (Brian M. Workman)

## Summary

mdv does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. Full stop.

## Information We Collect

None.

mdv is a markdown reader that operates entirely on your local device. It does not:

- Collect personal information of any kind
- Track usage, behavior, or analytics
- Use cookies, fingerprinting, or any form of identification
- Transmit data to us, our servers, or any third party
- Make network requests of any kind
- Load remote content
- Embed third-party analytics, advertising, or telemetry

The files you open and the content you read remain on your device and are never accessed, transmitted, or processed by us.

## Third-Party Services

mdv does not integrate with any third-party services. No analytics provider, advertising network, crash reporter, or cloud service receives any data from this application.

## Children's Privacy

Because mdv does not collect any information from anyone, it does not collect information from children. The application is suitable for users of all ages.

### Permissions

mdv may request operating-system permissions necessary to read the markdown files you choose to open. These permissions are used solely to display the content you select. No file contents, file names, paths, or metadata are transmitted off your device.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes — for instance, if a future version of mdv introduces functionality that involves data — this document will be updated and the effective date above will be revised. The current version of this policy applies to the version of mdv you are using.
